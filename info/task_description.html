On the chest keypad is a grid of numbered dots. The grid is comprised of a square shaped array of dots and contains lines that connect some pairs of adjacent dots. The answer to the code is the number of squares that are formed by these lines. For example, in the figure shown below, there are 3 squares: 2 small ones and 1 medium square.



The dots are marked by the numbers 1-16. The endpoints of the lines are represented by lists of two numbers.

Input: A list of lines as a list of lists. Each list consists of two integers.

Output: The quantity of squares formed as an integer.
